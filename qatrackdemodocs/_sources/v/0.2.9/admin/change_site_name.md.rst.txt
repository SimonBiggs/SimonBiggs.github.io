To change the name of your site from example.com:

1. From the drop down menu at the top right of the screen (by your
   username) select "Admin"
2. In the Sites section click on the "Sites" or "Change" link.
3. Click on the example.com site
4. Set the Domain name field to your server domain and root of your
   QATrack installation (e.g. ``yourcomputer/qatrack`` or
   ``123.456.1.1/qatrack``) and the Display name to what you want shown
   in the top left hand corner of your site.
5. Click Save
